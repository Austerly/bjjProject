About this website:

Regardless if you are the newest beginner, or an expert at the art of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, this website was created with you in mind. May this website serve you in the continuation of your Jiu Jitsu journey. While there are many videos on this website for learning and refining techniques, there is of course no substitution for mat time. As such, watching the videos embedded in this website is only the first step to effectively incorporating these techniques into your arsenal. The videos embedded in this website are all open-sourced from youtube, but organized for efficiency and convenience sake. Any recommendations for additional videos or resources that add additional insights are welcome. Please utilize the "Request a Video" link on the bottom left of this webpage to submit a recommendation.
